Take a look at the facilites we provide during your stay

The ground floor of the Spa building comprises of an indoor heated swimming pool with changing rooms, sauna, and Turkish steam room. You'll also find a rather pleasant private garden/sun patio with outdoor seating and lounging

Work up a sweat in our fully equipped gym. The upstairs of our Spa building houses a modern gym that accommodates all gym lovers. You'll find modern equipment and a welcoming atmosphere.

Lose yourself in our library perfect for the quieter moments. The Wellesley Park library is a peaceful setting stocked with a variety of books. We're sure you'll be able to find what you're looking for in there.

Our outdoor, all-weather tennis court is a real hit with our guests. Racquets and tennis balls are provided so that all our guests can take advantage of our outdoor, all-weather tennis court.
